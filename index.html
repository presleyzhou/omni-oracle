How the platform stays honest

Proper scoring everywhere

Every forecast — model, human, or market — is scored with Brier/log rules and public calibration curves (Gneiting's "sharpness subject to calibration"). The platform grades itself in the open.

Ensemble with attribution

Forecast combination per Timmermann (2006) with domain-fit extremizing (Satopää et al. 2014). Users see exactly which source — models, crowd, or markets — is winning in each domain.

Manipulation-resistant resolution

Three-tier resolution: automated feeds, optimistic oracle with bonds and dispute windows, and an expert council — designed around documented oracle-attack case studies.
